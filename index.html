Privacy Policy

SemmAI is a bilingual Tamil-English paraphrasing assistant designed to help users detect and generate paraphrases using AI. As an academic research project, we deeply value your privacy and are committed to protecting your data with the highest standards.

1. Data Storage

Your inputs, paraphrase outputs, and feedback are not processed through our backend services. No personally identifiable information is collected or stored. This ensures complete privacy protection for research participants.

2. Permissions

No permissions are required for using SemmAI. The application operates without accessing device storage, contacts, or other sensitive information.

3. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your input, usage, or feedback data with any third-party services. All AI processing is handled securely within our application infrastructure, maintaining academic research ethics and data protection standards.
